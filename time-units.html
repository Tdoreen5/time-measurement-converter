Understanding Time Units

Time is measured in different units depending on the scale we need. Here's a brief explanation of the most common time units:

Common Time Units

  • Seconds (s): The smallest standard unit of time. 1 second is the time it takes for a cesium atom to vibrate 9 billion times.
  • Minutes (min): 1 minute is equal to 60 seconds.
  • Hours (hr): 1 hour is equal to 60 minutes or 3600 seconds.
  • Days: 1 day is equal to 24 hours or 86400 seconds.
  • Weeks: 1 week is equal to 7 days.
  • Months: The length of a month varies (28 to 31 days). On average, a month is 30.44 days.
  • Years: 1 year is equal to 365 days, or 366 days in a leap year.
